# About Us

Biophysics applies physical principles to living systems, bridging life's complexity with the clarity of physical laws.

Biophysics is a hybrid science in which physical principles and methods are applied to the study of living organisms and the mechanics of life processes.

## Learning outcomes

During their Biophysics course, students will learn:

- Biophysical foundations of cellular structures, selected tissues and organs, and the whole body
- Physical processes in the human body
- Mechanisms and effects of physical factors on the human body
- Physical principles of modern diagnostic and therapeutic methods
